Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich fillings like ground meat (often beef or sausage), creamy ricotta cheese, mozzarella, and a flavorful tomato sauce, sometimes enriched with herbs like basil and oregano. These layers are repeated and typically topped with a generous amount of cheese that melts and browns in the oven, creating a golden, bubbly crust. The dish is baked until the flavors meld together, resulting in a comforting, savory meal with a deliciously hearty and cheesy texture.
